About Us

Punjabi Virsa is one of the most respected, disciplined and well maintained restaurants of India. We don't cook food to finish your hunger, we cook to make you fall in love for food.

We have some of the best team of waiters and chefs that have well experience of cooking and are capable of serving you the best.

We have a large variety of Indian, Chinese and Tandoori both veg and non-veg. Since we are well concerned with the difference of veg and non-veg, so we make sure to cook them separately.

The environment we provide to enjoy is like no where else, we not only have spacious furniture but a well relaxing atmosphere that makes you enjoy your food from heart.

The food and ambience are not the only reasons why people come to Punjabi Virsa. It is the quality of our service that we take pride in and our chefs and waiters are trained to go all out to make your Punjabi Virsa experience memorable. Feel free to ask them for any special requests that you might have and they will strive to address it as promptly and efficiently as possible.
